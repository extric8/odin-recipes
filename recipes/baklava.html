Baklava

Description

This dessert is made using various delicious ingredients including nuts, syrup, pastry sheets, and honey. Make this next time you have guests over and it will surely not disappoint.

Ingredients

  • 16 oz. package of phyllo dough
  • 1 pound chopped nuts
  • 1 cup butter
  • 1 tsp. ground cinnamon
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 tsp. vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup honey

Steps

  1. Obtain al ingredients.
  2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  3. Grease bottoms and sides of a 9x13 inch pan with butter or substitute
  4. Finely chop nuts and mix in cinnamon. Set aside.
  5. Unroll phyllo dough. Cut in half to fit to pan. Cover phyllo dough with a damp cloth to retain moisture. Place two sheets of dough in the pan butter thoroughly. Continue this until you have 8 layered sheets.
  6. Sprinkle 3 tablespoons of nut mixture on top. Addd two sheets of dough on top, layering with butter and nuts. The top layer should be 8 sheets deep.
  7. Cut dessert into diamond shapes and bake for approximately 50 minutes until the baklava is golden.
  8. While the baklava is baking, boil sugar and water until the sugar is melted. Add vanilla and honey, and simmer for 20 minutes. This will be the sauce.
  9. Remove the baklava from the oven and spoon the sauce over it.
  10. Enjoy this dessert!
