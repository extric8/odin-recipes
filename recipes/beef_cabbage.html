Ground Beef and Cabbage

Description

This dish will fill you up and it is a crowd pleaser. The beef is tender! Try this with a side of corn.

Ingredients

  • 1 large head of cabbage (chop finely)
  • 14.5 ounce can of diced tomatoes with juice
  • 1 onion (halve and thinly slice it)
  • 1 TBSP italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 pound of ground beef

Steps

  1. Obtain al ingredients.
  2. Place all ingredients except for the ground beef into a large pot or Dutch oven over low heat. Cook and stir regularly until it simmers.
  3. Add ground beef on top. Cover pot and cook until the cabbage is tender and until the ground beef is cooked. Stir occasionally. This should take about 45 minutes.
  4. Remove from heat and serve.
